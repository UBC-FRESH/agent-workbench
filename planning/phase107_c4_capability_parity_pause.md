# P107 C4 Capability-Parity Pause

## Decision

P107 is parked pending Phase 114. No further C4 or C4+ model comparison,
economic comparison, or P108 activation is authorized from the current
evidence.

## Why the C4 observations are not comparable

P113 established a narrow run-local adapter that can translate one native
`apply_patch(patch: string)` call for the configured Qwen Worker. Its five
fresh bounded trials are capability and reliability evidence for that exact
one-tool sandbox.

The later C4 observations did not activate the run-local adapter route. The
Worker instead encountered an unsupported native patch call, fell back to
shell-mediated writes, and failed deterministic validation. Those observations
remain useful negative integration evidence, but they do not isolate Ollama
Worker quality or cost against the paid C2 Worker.

## Outcome ledger

| Claim | Status | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| P113 narrow native-patch capability | accepted candidate | Deterministic adapter checks and bounded fresh trials passed. |
| C4 tool/session capability parity | unproven | The C4 route did not deploy the P113 adapter and did not reproduce the paid Worker tool contract. |
| C4 Worker quality | not comparable | Transport and tool-surface failure preceded a valid implementation evaluation. |
| C4 protocol acceptance | false | Required worktree artifacts and deterministic checks failed. |
| C4 economics | unusable | Invalid treatment observations cannot support an economic delta. |

## Publication-validity boundary

A future P107 comparison must predeclare the exact C2/C4 capability contract,
freeze the effective configuration and workload before new live outcomes, and
retain every invalid observation in a transparent ledger. A bridge, host,
configuration, root-binding, history, or artifact-flow failure invalidates the
affected observation; it is not counted as an Ollama model-quality or cost
loss.

P114 owns that prerequisite. It will determine whether the current
Codex/Ollama route is viable for the declared C4 workload, or produce a
bounded negative decision packet that identifies the failed capability and
rules out a comparable P107 lane on this route.

## Consolidation boundary

The P107 mid-phase consolidation may include validated research contracts,
offline validators, frozen workload definitions, and this pause record. It
must exclude raw sessions, provider traffic, local credentials, failed C4
candidate code, temporary files, and unvalidated artifacts.
